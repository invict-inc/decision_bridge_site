Our solid conviction is that if a business is to be upgraded, it's to be streamlined in a full cycle way!

DecisionBridge is a development consulting organization based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania with the objective of contributing to the improvement of people’s lives through supporting evidence-based decision making. We support the decision making processes of development actors by assisting them to gather the evidence needed, analyzing it, and making sense of it through research, monitoring, and evaluation. Drawing from our name, we support development actors to bridge the divide between data and decision making through the provision of M&E solutions, build the internal capacity of the actors in M&E and Learning, and in the operationalization of Results-Based Management Systems. In particular, we have skills and experience to design and roll-out simple and practical MEL and RBM systems on behalf of development actors. In this, we work in close collaboration with development actors during MEL design phase and, coach and mentor their staff throughout the process in the practical application of MEL tools in a real-world situations. In addition, we have a proven track record of institutional capacity strengthening in MEL and in overall organizational development aspects. We build the capacity of the organizations in grants management, financial management skills, value for money assessments, data quality checks/audits. Also, we support corporate (business) sector performance through culture transformation of its workforce and analytics whereby we transform messy corporate data into final work products whose conclusions are clear for decision making. DecisionBridge Ltd was registered in May 2012 in Tanzania with a Certificate of Incorporation number 90883, Tax Identification number 117-538-893, and Business License number B-01396208. Our offices are located on the second floor, GdB House, Block H Plot 2040, Off Bagamoyo Road, Tegeta, Kinondoni.
